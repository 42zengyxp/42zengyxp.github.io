A few evenings ago I watched the sun setting behind the mountains. As it lowered in the western sky, its long level rays lit up the green fields of wheat and all the trees and bushes with a golden glowed making everything very clear and distinct. The clouds above the sky began to glow with a golden light, and the mountains were of a lovely purple. The white house a little distance away looked as though they were made of silver.

HOW I FIRST MET JOHN

Well, we met at a school in Pokhara. You see I'd just moved to Pokhara because of my job and I didn't really know anybody. There was a program at school. I had to go there. People were talking to each other in their local language. I didn't understand them very much. And I was feeling bored. And then I noticed a boy who looked, as bored as I was. Then I went close to him and we introduced each other. He was John. He had come there with his uncle to spend his holidays. And that's how it all started really.

MY EARLIEST CHILDHOOD MEMORY

It was eleven years ago. I was just five then. My father took me to a cinema. He had a piece of paper at a window. We went to a gate. It was closed. When he showed the paper to a man at the gate, he allowed us go in. It was completely dark inside. There was a black curtain hanging at the gate. The gatekeeper had a torchlight in his hand. He led us to a seat. My father sat on it and I sat on his lap. In front there was a colorful scene. My father told me the story of the film. And I was watching the story in front of my eyes. And I was very happy.
