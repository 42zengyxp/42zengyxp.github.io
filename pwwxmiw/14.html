14.5 JUST IN CASE

1.     

A: You'd better stop for petrol.

B: Why?

A: Well, you never know.  You might run out of petrol.

B: Oh, that's all right- I'll fill at the next garage.

A: Well, I think you should fill right now anyway, just in case there is not another garage.

2.     

A: You'd better buy some candles.

B: Why?

A: Well, you never know. There might be load shedding.

B: Oh, that's all right - I’ll buy them at the corner shop.

A: Well, I think you should buy them here anyway, just in case the corner shop is closed.

3.

14.7 GENERAL ADVICE

You should leave plenty of time in case you get lost in the darkness. You ought to carry some food with you so that you do not go hungry. It is a good idea to listen to the weather forecast before you start out because otherwise you might be in danger. You should wear suitable clothing and boots so that you are protected. You ought to take a map and compass so that you do not get lost. It's best to go in a group of at least four in case you need to help one another in difficulty. You should never go alone because if you do so you might risk your life. It's a good idea to tell somebody where you are going because they might help you if necessary. It's best to wear a bright-coloured anorak so that people can locate you easily.

Give general advice to other people.

a.    If you go to live in a tropical country, you should wear light cotton clothes so that your body is not wet. You ought to use an ointment or a net to keep mosquitoes away because otherwise you might suffer from malaria. It's a good idea to drink boiled water so that you don't suffer from diarrhoea and dysentery. It is best never to walk in the dark without a torchlight in case a poisonous snake bites you. You should take necessary medicine with you so that it is not available there. You should never walk in the midday sun in summer so that you don't get affected by heat wave.

b.    If you drive across the Sahara Desert, you should take enough water and food for you. You ought to fill in the tank and take spare tyre so that you don't have to suffer. It's a good idea not to go alone because it might be dangerous and you might be a helpless condition. It's best to travel from one oasis to another in case you run out of water. You should never drive in the sandstorm so that you don't breathe in the thick sandy air. You ought to travel at night because you might be safe from heat.

c.     If you yacht at sea, you should listen to the weather forecast before you start out because you might learn about the wind. You ought to learn to read the signs of weather so that you don't suffer in the storm. It's a good idea to study the charts because you might reach the destination safely. It's best to take enough food and drink so that you don't die of hunger. You should also take a map and a compass so that you don' t miss the right direction.

d.    If you ski it's best to listen to the weather forecast so that you don't have to come back without skiing in very cold weather. You ought not to choose the very steep slope in case you risk your life. It is best to fasten skiing equipment well so that you can complete your skiing safely. It's a good idea for the learner not to try ski jump because it might be dangerous. You ought to learn to ski before going to the hill so that you don't miss enjoying it.
