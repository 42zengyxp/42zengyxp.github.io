8.4 THE PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS

1.    He's been working on his first novel.

2.    He's thought of a good title, and he’s made a list of characters and he's designed the front cover.

3.    He's written two pages so far. He hasn't decided what happens next.


4.    Present Perfect Continuous is used to describe general activities which may have been stopped now or still going on. Present Perfect Simple is used to describe the individual things which have been achieved during the given period.

8.7 A BUSY TIME

1.    I've been getting my life organized recently. I've made a routine to do everything from morning to evening. Yesterday I went to bed at 10 and this morning I got up at five and went to college at 6. After I came back home. In the afternoon I went to the library, I've prepared one-third of English textbook. I've also been doing economics and math. I've not missed any important programs on TV. Last Saturday I went on a picnic with my friends. I haven't been to the restaurant in the evenings with my friends. I've regularly done my homework.

2.    I've been finding out about holiday jobs over the past couple of weeks. I've not got any job to earn money. Since I came here, I've tried to get all kinds of jobs. I tried to find out the work as a waiter in a hotel. I showed my interest in the kitchen work in a restaurant. I came to this town to get a job and to earn money to enjoy my holiday. I've almost finished the money I brought with me. If I didn't manage any job in a day or two, I'd have no choice except returning home. I haven't even got an outdoor job. I've passed my days visiting different places and meeting different people, but I haven’t got a job yet.

3.    I've been keeping very fit lately. I've started constant and regular exercise. I haven't felt anything about bodily pain and weakness. Before doing exercise, I used to be very dull and I was not interested in my life. I was very unhappy. My working power failed. I felt that my life was a punishment. But these days been walking regularly for one hour a day. I have also been playing football and cricket. I’ve felt fresh when I wake up in the morning. My life has been changing a lot. Although I am poor, my life has become rich.

8.8 RECENT DEVELOPMENTS

Free practice

1.    I live in Muglin. This town has been expanding for twenty years. In the beginning there were only five hotels and nothing else. But now it has more than one hundred hotels. Three petrol pumps have been doing their services. One high school and three primary schools have been established. In the past only a few vehicles stopped there. But now almost every vehicle has started to stop there. Almost two hundred- night buses have been feeding their passengers. This town also has showed some bad signs which have to be checked. The people of that area have been building a hospital there. It has still been attracting more and more people.

2.    My country has been enjoying democracy since 2046 BS.  A democratic constitution has come into effect. General election has taken place five times. The government has been changing frequently. The leaders have shown their love for the chair. Nobody has been thinking of the country. Because of instability, business and industry have not developed properly. Prices have been raising higher and higher. People have started to blame the leaders of all parties for being selfish. Nothing has been done yet to fulfil the basic needs of the people. But the leaders have done everything in speech. Now people have changed a lot.  They have become more conscious. They can't be easily deceived by the sweet speeches. They need something solid from their leader.  

3.    The scene of the world has been changing every day. The dictators have come into pawer and gone out of it. Many Asian countries have been developing their economic conditions. Civil War has broken out in one country or the other, and the people have left their countries. They have lived in other countries as such refugees have become a burden to the host nations. The people of today have become more conscious of environment and wild life. They have been making progress in every field. Computer technology has changed the quality of human life greatly. The internet and E-mail have made the world like a small home.

Life isn't a bed of roses. There are many ups and downs in life. If you're happy today, don't think that you'll be the same tomorrow, if you're in trouble you think of your friend.
