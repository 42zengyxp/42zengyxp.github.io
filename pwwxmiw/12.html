Writing

A Boeing 747 was flying over the Atlantic on its way from London to New York when it was hijacked by a man covering his face with black cloth last night. The high-jacker instructed the pilot to land the plane at Tribhuvan International Airport and demanded the release of 10 prisoners in Tihar Jail. Fortunately, SSP Narayan brought the hijacker under control and no one was injured.


150 demonstrators were arrested at Jaulakhel yesterday. They were taking part in an anti-nuclear Demonstration. They were going to the American Embassy to submit a protest letter to the American Ambassador. Their main intention was to stop the construction of the nuclear power plant which is being built in the South Asian Region.


The Egyptian Ambassador's son was going to Lincoln School at Ravi Bhavan yesterday at nine in the morning. At Soaltee bend his car was forced to stop. Three gunmen came out of a blue car and took the boy out and took him in their car. Their car headed towards Kalanki. According to the eye witness, all this was completed within a minute and there were no police nearby at that time.


The Education Minister was making a speech at Public Campus Dhulabari. He was promising to provide many facilities to the students. All of a sudden, a group of students started throwing tomatoes at him. They claimed that the MPs were enjoying the facilities of duty- free cars whereas the students had to pay customs on their text-books.


A man with a black briefcase was trying to go through the customs at Heathrow Airport. He was trying pass without showing the briefcase to the Customs Officer. When the officer checked it, he found something black and he called the police who informed that it was a bomb and the man was arrested immediately. The police have suspected him to be a member of IRA and that he intended to explode a Boeing 747.


Ram Thapa was playing golf at Royal Nepal Golf Club at four yesterday. The sky was covered with black clouds. As he was near a tree he was struck by lightning and he dropped unconscious. The caddie and the ball boy rushed to him and took him to Bir Hospital. Now he is undergoing treatment in the ICU of the hospital and he's not out of danger.

2.    A couple of weekends ago a woman was walking on the footpath in the country. It went through the field. She was so busy looking out for the footpath that she didn't notice the bulls in the field. Seeing her bright red coat, they started to chase her. She was scared very much but she saved her life jumping over the nearest fence.

B: Yes, I have.

12.7 RUPERT AND THE SPACE PIRATES

One evening Rupert was sitting in his porch admiring the night sky when he saw three bright things in the sky. To his surprise they were flying close to his house. He could see that they were flying saucers.


Next day, the flying saucers landed outside the town and strange creatures came out of them. They started chasing the people. The people went to hide in a safer place. These aliens started looting. They started collecting different things from the houses. They also robbed the bank.


That night Rupert was hiding in the bush and watching their activities through his binoculars. He could see one of the aliens opening a box. As it opened the box, a mouse came out and he could see the mouse shrieking at it, the creature was frightened. An idea came into Rupert’s mind.


Next evening, he went near the flying saucers. He had collected a lot of mice in a trap. When opened the trap they ran towards the aliens. They felt that their lives were in danger, so they hurriedly flew away in their flying saucers. All the people got rid of the creatures and were very happy.

Composition

The society today has been highly industrialized and people become busier. A man has no time to take interest in another man's activities. People can't share their happiness and sorrows. A man feels lovely even in the crowd of the city. Although a man has become richer and more comfortable, he is not as happy as he was in the past.


Because of the freedom, the young people have not been able to control themselves and to make their future better. They don't have to work as hard as their grandparents used to. They have become selfish and materialistic. They don't respect their senior. They think that they know all. In the past pollutants were few, so it was not necessary to be careful about environment. But today in spite of great care, the degree of pollution is increasing every day. People have become richer, so they don't value education as

much as they did in the past. Therefore, the general standard of education has become lower.
