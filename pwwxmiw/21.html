21.5 FAULTS AND REMEDIES

1.     

A: I think the trouble with leisure facilities in our area lies with the local leaders. I don' t think the leaders take nearly enough interest in the activities of the young people. I think the young people go to the tea-shop and pass the whole morning talking uselessly. They don't have enough exercise.

B: Yes, I agree. I think the young people are too careless about their future. They don't study seriously. Neither do they help their parents with their household work.


A: I think all the leaders should try to establish a club so that all the young people can’t pass their time doing one thing or the other. I think the young people should be encouraged to take interest in practical life.

2.     

A: The trouble with my school lies with the teacher. I don't think they get nearly enough training in how to teach. I think they neglect the subject matter and they talk about politics in the classroom.

B: Yes, I agree. I think they are very much active at the time of election. I don't think they are worried about the decreasing quality of the students.

A: I think all the teachers should not be involved in politics. I think they should be forced either to take interest in teaching or to go to politics leaving the schools.

3.     

A: The trouble with out local transport services lies with the bus drivers. I don’t think they should wait for passengers at ever stop. If all of them go on without waiting they will get the equal number of passengers.

B: Yes, I agree. I think they start the engines of their buses even when wait for a Ion time. I think their carelessness is responsible for air pollution.

A: I think all the drivers should be allowed to stop their buses for not more than one- minute stop. I think the traffic policeman at every major stop should be asked to check whether the engines have been started or not.

4.     

A: The trouble with TV programs lies with commercials. I don't think they should broadcast these commercials by breaking the programs. I think we find it quite difficult to understand the episode or message clearly.  

B: Yes, I we find it quite difficult to understand the episode or agree. I think we are all confused about the commercials and the episodes. And that means we are wasting time watching advertisement. I think we are forced to buy what they want us to buy, not what we really need to buy.

A: I think these commercials should be banned if possible but if it's not possible then they should be broadcast before and after the particular programs.

a.    Hold or keep.

6.    Venus is mysterious, we don't know much about it because it's covered in cloud, it's traditionally compared to a woman (or goddess).

7.    If Venusians existed, they would have fair-hair. They would chase across the planet in flying saucers.

Writing

The sunlight reaching Venus is twice as powerful as that reaching the Earth.One long Venusian 'day' is equal to 59 days on earth.  At that time the temperature reaches 450 degrees Centigrade. It is easily hot enough to melt tin or lead. Venus has an atmosphere about 100 times dense as ours. The atmosphere is so thick that you could fly through it without any problem. A seven mile per hour wind there would be strong enough to knock down a tall building here. Because of the clouds of sulphur and sulphuric acid, the Sun's light there is no more than a dark reddish gloom. The light came not from the Sun but from flashes of lightning given off by continual electrical storms. All in all, then Venus turns out to be dramatic though extremely inhospitable.
