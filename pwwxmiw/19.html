But staying at home most of the time will make life dull. They will hunger for the society and the crowd.

4.    Singers will no longer sing on the stage They will sit in a studio and sing. People will sit in their own rooms and watch them singing on the computer screen. The telephone will connect the studio and the computer. The audience will ask the singers to sing their favorite songs. But it will be difficult for the singers to fulfil their audience's demand. Since people don't see others in person, life will look more mechanical.

5.    In the computerized world of tomorrow students will not go into the classrooms. Teachers will give their lectures sitting in front of the computer, and students will ask the teachers on the screen what they don't understand. The students will be able to read any book in any library in the world. Their computers will help them to find the necessary reading material. The computers themselves will guide the students what to write and how. But it will make many teachers jobless. The students may be lazier because they won' t have to work so hard.
